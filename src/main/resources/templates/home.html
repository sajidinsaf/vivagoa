Our Story

A Taste of Goa in Every Bite

Welcome to Viva Goa — a beloved dining destination nestled in Colva, South Goa. For over 24 years, we have been serving authentic Goan cuisine, fresh seafood, and a rich selection of Continental, Chinese, and Indian dishes.

Our restaurant captures the essence of Goan hospitality — warm, vibrant, and full of flavor. From our signature fish curry rice to sizzling seafood platters, every dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you are a traveler seeking an authentic Goan dining experience or a local looking for your favorite comfort food, Viva Goa promises a meal that stays with you long after the last bite.
